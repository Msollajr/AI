Question: "How do I register for courses?"

Answer
Answer:
Course registration is done online via the Student Portal during the first two weeks of the semester. To register: log in to your portal, navigate to 'Course Registration', select your required and elective courses based on your curriculum sheet, and click 'Submit'. You must consult your Academic Advisor before finalizing. The deadline for adding or dropping courses is the Friday of the second week of lectures.
