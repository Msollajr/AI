What are the hostel application procedures?
Answer:
On-campus hostel accommodation is allocated on a first-come, first-served basis, with priority given to first-year undergraduate and international students. Applications open online on the Student Portal one month before the semester starts. You must pay a non-refundable application fee first, select your preferred hostel block, and upload your proof of payment.

What you should do:
1. Log in to the Student Portal.
2. Navigate to 'Hostel Application'.
3. Pay the non-refundable application fee.
4. Select your preferred hostel block.
5. Upload proof of payment.
