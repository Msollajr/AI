# Graduation Requirements 2025/2026

## Overview
Graduation from the University of Dar es Salaam requires completion of all academic, financial, and administrative requirements as specified in the University Regulations. Students must apply for graduation during the published application period.

## Academic Requirements for Graduation

### Undergraduate Programmes
- Complete the **minimum required credit units**: 120 units (3-year programmes) or 160 units (4-year programmes).
- Achieve a minimum **Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) of 2.0** at the time of graduation.
- Pass all **core/compulsory courses** with a minimum grade of **D (40%)**.
- Complete and submit the **final year project or dissertation** — approved by the Department.
- Complete any required **industrial attachment** or field practicum and submit the attachment report.
- Have no outstanding **incomplete (I) grades** — all must be resolved before the graduation deadline.

### Master's Programmes
- Complete all coursework with a minimum CGPA of **3.0**.
- Complete and pass the **dissertation** (research programmes) or final project (coursework programmes).
- All research dissertations must be approved by the **Internal and External Examiners**.
- Submit the final bound copy of the dissertation to the Directorate of Postgraduate Studies.

### PhD Programmes
- Complete and pass the **PhD thesis**, which must demonstrate original contribution to knowledge.
- Successfully defend the thesis in a viva voce examination before a panel of examiners.
- Incorporate all examiner corrections within the specified timeframe.
- Submit the final bound copy to the Directorate of Postgraduate Studies.

## Financial and Administrative Requirements
- Clear **all tuition and examination fees** — no outstanding balance.
- Clear **all library fines** — library clearance certificate required.
- Clear **hostel dues** (if a hostel resident) — hostel clearance certificate required.
- Return all **borrowed library books**.
- No pending **disciplinary cases** — if any case is under investigation, graduation is deferred until resolved.
- Submit the completed **Graduation Clearance Form** signed by the Library, Bursar, Dean of School, and Dean of Students.

## Graduation Clearance Process
1. Log in to the Student Portal (portal.udsm.ac.tz).
2. Click on **Graduation Application** — available 2 months before the graduation ceremony.
3. Check that all your results and credit units are correctly recorded — any discrepancy must be resolved with the Academic Registrar before applying.
4. Generate and pay the **Graduation Fee: Tsh 100,000** (covers gown hire, certificate, programme booklet).
5. Collect the **Graduation Clearance Form** from the Academic Registrar's Office.
6. Get clearance signatures from:
   - Library (Room L.01, Main Library)
   - Bursar's Office (Admin Building, Ground Floor)
   - Dean of your School / College Principal
   - Dean of Students Office (Admin Building, Ground Floor)
7. Submit the completed form to the Academic Registrar's Office.
8. Await confirmation of your inclusion in the graduation list (published 4 weeks before the ceremony).

## Graduation Ceremonies
| Ceremony | Month | Venue | Who Attends |
|---|---|---|---|
| First Graduation | January | Nkrumah Hall, Main Campus | Students who completed by end of Semester 1 |
| Second Graduation | July | Nkrumah Hall, Main Campus | Students who completed by end of Semester 2 |

- **Graduation application deadline**: 2 months before the ceremony.
- Graduates and their guests (maximum 4 tickets per graduate) may attend the ceremony.
- **Dress code**: Academic gown and mortarboard (hired from the university on graduation day).
- Gown hire is included in the Tsh 100,000 graduation fee.
- Guests attending must be formally dressed.

## Degree Certificate
- Degree certificates are issued at the graduation ceremony.
- Students unable to attend may collect the certificate in person from the Academic Registrar's Office **30 days after the ceremony**.
- Certificates can also be collected by an authorised representative (with a notarised power of attorney).
- **Lost certificate replacement**: Apply to the Academic Registrar in writing with a police abstract. Fee: Tsh 50,000. Processing time: 6–8 weeks.

## Degree Verification
- Employers and institutions wishing to verify a UDSM degree can contact: registrar@udsm.ac.tz.
- Online verification (for authorised institutions): https://registrar.udsm.ac.tz/verify
- Verification letter for the graduate: Tsh 10,000 — apply at the Academic Registrar's Office.

## Posthumous Degree
- In exceptional circumstances, a posthumous degree may be awarded to a student who passed away while enrolled and had completed substantial requirements.
- Apply through the Dean of Students Office on behalf of the family.

## Common Questions About Graduation

**Q: When and where are graduation ceremonies held?**
A: Two ceremonies per year — January and July — at Nkrumah Hall on the Main Campus. January is for students completing Semester 1; July for Semester 2 completers.

**Q: How much is the graduation fee and what does it cover?**
A: The graduation fee is **Tsh 100,000**. It covers your degree certificate, academic gown hire, mortarboard, and programme booklet.

**Q: What is the minimum CGPA to graduate?**
A: Undergraduate: minimum CGPA of **2.0**. Master's: minimum CGPA of **3.0**. PhD: based on thesis examiners' approval — no numeric CGPA minimum.

**Q: What happens if I have outstanding library fines at graduation time?**
A: You cannot be cleared for graduation until all library fines are paid and all borrowed books returned. Pay at the Library Circulation Desk or via the Student Portal.

**Q: I missed the graduation ceremony. Can I still get my certificate?**
A: Yes. Your certificate will be available for collection from the Academic Registrar's Office 30 days after the ceremony. Alternatively, send an authorised representative with a notarised power of attorney.

**Q: My degree certificate was lost or damaged. How do I get a replacement?**
A: Submit a written application to the Academic Registrar with a police abstract (report of loss). Pay Tsh 50,000. Processing takes 6–8 weeks.
