# Graduation Requirements 2025/2026

## Undergraduate Programmes
- Complete minimum of 120 credit units (3-year programme) or 160 credit units (4-year programme)
- Achieve minimum CGPA of 2.0
- Complete all compulsory/core courses for the programme
- Complete internship/industrial attachment (minimum 8 weeks)
- Complete final year research project/dissertation
- No outstanding fees or library obligations
- No pending disciplinary cases

## Master's Programmes
- Complete minimum of 60 credit units (coursework) or 40 credit units (research)
- Achieve minimum CGPA of 3.0
- Complete and defend dissertation/thesis
- Publish at least one paper from the research (for research master's)
- Submit bound copies of dissertation (3 copies)

## PhD Programmes
- Complete minimum of 20 credit units (coursework if required)
- Complete and defend doctoral thesis
- Publish at least two papers from the research in peer-reviewed journals
- Submit bound copies of thesis (5 copies)
- Satisfy all publication requirements of the college

## Graduation Application
- Apply online via student portal during the graduation application period
- Graduation fee: Tsh 100,000
- Application deadline: 2 months before the graduation ceremony
- Late application: Tsh 150,000 (within 1 month of ceremony)

## Graduation Ceremonies
- Held twice per year: January and July
- Venue: Nkrumah Hall
- Gown hire: Tsh 50,000 (inclusive of cap and gown)
- Photography: Tsh 30,000 (professional photographers available on site)
- Guests: Maximum 4 guests per graduand

## Transcript and Certificates
- Official transcript: Tsh 20,000 per copy
- Certificate replacement: Tsh 50,000 (lost or damaged)
- Processing time: 2-4 weeks after graduation
- Collect from: Academic Registrar's Office
