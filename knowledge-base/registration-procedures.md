# Registration Procedures 2025/2026

## New Student Registration
1. Check admission status on the student portal
2. Accept admission offer online within 2 weeks
3. Pay acceptance fee of Tsh 50,000
4. Upload required documents: 
   - Birth certificate
   - Academic certificates (secondary school, diploma, etc.)
   - Passport-sized photographs (2)
   - Medical clearance report
5. Receive student registration number
6. Pay tuition fees (first installment minimum 60%)
7. Register for courses online via student portal
8. Attend orientation week

## Continuing Student Registration
1. Log in to student portal
2. Clear any outstanding fees from previous semester
3. Pay current semester fees (minimum 60%)
4. Select courses for the semester (consult Academic Advisor)
5. Submit registration
6. Print registration slip
7. Attend classes

## Course Selection Guidelines
- Consult your Academic Advisor before selecting courses
- Follow your programme curriculum sheet
- Maximum 7 courses (21 credits) per semester
- Minimum 4 courses (12 credits) per semester
- Prerequisites must be completed before enrolling in advanced courses
- Some courses have limited capacity - register early

## Registration Statuses
- **Registered**: Full registration completed, eligible to attend classes and exams
- **Provisional**: Fees partially paid, restricted access to some services
- **Pending**: Documents under review, not yet fully registered
- **Incomplete**: Missing required documents or payments
- **Withdrawn**: Student has withdrawn from the semester

## Transfer and Interruption
- Transfer between programmes: Apply to Academic Registrar, requires minimum CGPA of 2.5
- Interruption of studies: Maximum 2 semesters, apply to Dean of School
- Readmission: Required if interruption exceeds 2 semesters
- Transfer from other universities: Credit transfer evaluated by the relevant School Board
