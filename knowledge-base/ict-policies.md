# ICT Policies and Services 2025/2026

## Overview
The Directorate of ICT Services manages all technology infrastructure at UDSM including campus Wi-Fi, student email, the Student Portal, computer labs, and the Moodle Learning Management System. Students are expected to use all ICT resources responsibly and for academic purposes only.

## Campus Wi-Fi

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Network Name (SSID) | UniStudent-WiFi |
| Username | Your student registration number (e.g., 2024-04-09876) |
| Password | Your Student Portal password |
| Max devices per student | 3 devices |
| Speed per student | 10 Mbps during academic hours |
| Coverage | All academic buildings, library, hostels, and Students' Centre |

**How to connect:**
1. Select **UniStudent-WiFi** from your device's Wi-Fi list.
2. Enter your registration number as the username.
3. Enter your portal password.
4. Accept the acceptable use policy if prompted.

If you cannot connect, reset your portal password first via the ICT Help Desk self-service portal.

## Student Email
- **Format**: `registrationnumber@student.udsm.ac.tz` (e.g., `2024-04-09876@student.udsm.ac.tz`)
- **Mailbox storage**: 50 GB
- **Webmail access**: https://mail.student.udsm.ac.tz
- **Important**: All official university communications (exam results, registration notices, fee statements) are sent **only** to your student email. Check it regularly.
- **Forwarding**: You may forward to Gmail or Outlook but the official address must remain active.

## Student Portal
- **URL**: https://portal.udsm.ac.tz
- **Services available on the portal**:
  - Course registration and timetable
  - Fee statement and payment Control Number
  - Examination results
  - Hostel application
  - Supplementary exam application
  - Transcript request
  - Academic calendar and announcements
- **Password reset**: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the portal login page (requires security questions set up at registration), or visit the ICT Help Desk in person.

## Moodle Learning Management System (LMS)
- **URL**: https://elearning.udsm.ac.tz
- **Purpose**: Lecturers upload course materials, assignments, quizzes, and announcements on Moodle.
- **Login**: Use the same credentials as the Student Portal (registration number + portal password).
- **How to enrol in a course**: After registering for courses on the portal, your Moodle enrolment is automatic within 24 hours. If missing, contact your lecturer or the ICT Help Desk.
- **Assignments**: Submit assignments via Moodle before the deadline set by the lecturer — late submissions are penalised according to the course policy.
- **Mobile app**: Moodle is accessible via the **Moodle Mobile** app (available on Android and iOS) using the server URL elearning.udsm.ac.tz.

## Video Conferencing (Zoom & Microsoft Teams)
- UDSM provides licensed **Zoom** accounts to students for online lectures and group meetings.
- Access: Log in at https://udsm-ac-tz.zoom.us using your student email.
- **Microsoft Teams** is also available for collaborative work and is integrated with student email.
- Access Teams via https://teams.microsoft.com using your @student.udsm.ac.tz email.

## Off-Campus Access and VPN
- Many library databases and e-resources require campus network access.
- **VPN setup for off-campus access**:
  1. Download the UDSM VPN client from https://ict.udsm.ac.tz/vpn
  2. Install and open the VPN client.
  3. Enter server address: `vpn.udsm.ac.tz`
  4. Login with your student registration number and portal password.
  5. Once connected, you can access all library databases and e-resources from anywhere.
- **Supported platforms**: Windows, macOS, Android, iOS.

## Acceptable Use Policy
All students must comply with the following:
- University internet and systems are for **academic and official purposes only**.
- **No downloading** of pirated software, movies, music, or copyrighted materials.
- **No accessing** inappropriate, illegal, or offensive content.
- **No hacking**, port scanning, or attempting unauthorized access to any university system.
- **No sharing** of your login credentials with any other person.
- Violations are logged and may result in internet access suspension, disciplinary action, and in serious cases, referral to law enforcement.

## Common Questions About ICT

**Q: How do I connect to campus Wi-Fi?**
A: Select UniStudent-WiFi, use your registration number as username and your portal password. If it doesn't work, reset your password at the ICT Help Desk or via the portal's Forgot Password link.

**Q: I forgot my student portal password. How do I reset it?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on portal.udsm.ac.tz and answer your security questions. If that doesn't work, visit the ICT Help Desk at Science Building Ground Floor Room G.01 with your student ID.

**Q: How do I access Moodle?**
A: Go to elearning.udsm.ac.tz and log in with your registration number and portal password. Your courses appear automatically after registration, within 24 hours.

**Q: How do I access library databases from home?**
A: Download and connect to the UDSM VPN from ict.udsm.ac.tz/vpn using your student credentials. Once connected, access all library e-resources as if you were on campus.

**Q: What is my student email address?**
A: It is your registration number followed by @student.udsm.ac.tz (e.g., 2024-04-09876@student.udsm.ac.tz). Access it at mail.student.udsm.ac.tz.
