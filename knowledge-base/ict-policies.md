# ICT Policies and Services 2025/2026

## Campus Wi-Fi
- Network name: UniStudent-WiFi
- Username: Student registration number
- Password: Portal password (same as student portal)
- Connection limit: Up to 3 devices per student
- Speed: 10 Mbps per student during academic hours

## Student Email
- Format: registrationnumber@student.udsm.ac.tz
- Storage: 50 GB mailbox
- Access: Webmail at mail.student.udsm.ac.tz
- Official university communications sent ONLY to student email

## Portal Access
- URL: https://portal.udsm.ac.tz
- Services: Course registration, fee checking, exam results, hostel application
- Password reset: Via ICT Help Desk or self-service security questions

## Computer Labs
- Lab Locations: Science Building (Ground Floor), Library (1st Floor), Engineering Building
- Operating Hours: 7:00 AM - 9:00 PM daily
- Printing: Tsh 100 per page (black and white), Tsh 500 per page (color)
- Scanning: Free
- Rules: No food or drinks, no gaming, no unauthorized software installation

## ICT Support Desk
- Location: Science Building, Ground Floor, Room G.01
- Hours: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM weekdays, 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM Saturdays
- Email: support@udsm.ac.tz
- Phone: +255 22 2410700
- Response time: Within 24 hours for non-emergency tickets

## Acceptable Use Policy
- University internet is for academic purposes only
- No downloading of copyrighted materials
- No access to inappropriate content
- No hacking or unauthorized network access
- Violations result in internet access suspension and disciplinary action
