# Faculty and Department Information 2025/2026

## Overview
The University of Dar es Salaam is organized into 9 Colleges and Schools, each with departments offering undergraduate and postgraduate programmes. Each College is headed by a Principal, and each School by a Dean. Departments are headed by Heads of Department (HoDs).

## Academic Staff Office Hours
- Lecturers are required to maintain office hours of minimum **2 hours per week** for student consultations.
- Office hours are posted on Moodle (elearning.udsm.ac.tz) and on department notice boards at the start of each semester.
- For queries outside office hours: email the lecturer using their @udsm.ac.tz address.

## Head of Department (HoD) Contacts
Contact the relevant Head of Department for:
- Programme information and curriculum enquiries
- Course exemption requests
- Academic recommendation letters
- Research supervision requests
- Departmental elective course availability

## Dean of School / Principal of College
Contact the Dean or Principal for:
- Programme transfer requests
- Interruption of studies approval
- Appeals against departmental decisions
- Academic misconduct hearings

## Common Questions About Faculties

**Q: Which college offers Computer Science at UDSM?**
A: Computer Science is offered at the College of Information and Communication Technologies (CoICT). Contact: coict@udsm.ac.tz | Website: www.coict.udsm.ac.tz.

**Q: Which school offers Law (LLB) at UDSM?**
A: The School of Law (SoL) offers the LLB programme. 4-year programme for direct entry students, 3-year for graduates. Contact: law@udsm.ac.tz.

**Q: How do I find my lecturer's office hours?**
A: Office hours are posted on Moodle (elearning.udsm.ac.tz) at the start of each semester and also on notice boards outside department offices.

**Q: Who do I contact for programme transfer or interruption?**
A: Contact the Dean of your School (for interruption) or the Academic Registrar (for programme transfer between schools). The Dean of Students Office (deanstudents@udsm.ac.tz) can also guide you.

**Q: I need a recommendation letter from my lecturer. How do I request it?**
A: Email your lecturer directly at their @udsm.ac.tz email address, providing the purpose, deadline, and any forms required. Give at least 2 weeks' notice.
