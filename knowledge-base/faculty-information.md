- **College of Humanities**: History, Literature, Languages, Philosophy, Fine Arts
- **College of Natural and Applied Sciences**: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Geology

- **College of Business**: Accounting, Finance, Marketing, Management, Entrepreneurship

- Meetings: Minimum 2 per semester
- Purpose: Course selection, academic progress review, career guidance
- Change of advisor: Apply to Dean of School
