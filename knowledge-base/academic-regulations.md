# Academic Regulations 2025/2026

## Course Registration
- Registration opens 2 weeks before semester start, closes end of Week 2
- Students must register for a minimum of 4 courses (12 credits) and maximum of 7 courses (21 credits) per semester
- Adding/dropping courses permitted during first 2 weeks of lectures
- Late registration incurs a penalty fee of Tsh 50,000
- All registrations must be approved by the Academic Advisor

## Grading System
- A: 70-100% (Excellent) - 5.0 grade points
- B+: 60-69% (Very Good) - 4.5 grade points  
- B: 55-59% (Good) - 4.0 grade points
- C+: 50-54% (Average) - 3.5 grade points
- C: 45-49% (Satisfactory) - 3.0 grade points
- D: 40-44% (Pass) - 2.0 grade points
- E: 35-39% (Marginal Fail) - 1.0 grade point
- F: 0-34% (Fail) - 0.0 grade points

## Progression Requirements
- GPA >= 3.5: Good standing, eligible for Dean's List
- GPA 2.0 - 3.4: Satisfactory standing
- GPA 1.5 - 1.9: Probation - student must retake failed courses
- GPA < 1.5: Discontinuation - student may not continue in the program for one academic year
- A student on probation for two consecutive semesters will be discontinued

## Degree Classification
- First Class: CGPA 4.4 - 5.0
- Upper Second Class: CGPA 3.5 - 4.3
- Lower Second Class: CGPA 2.7 - 3.4
- Pass: CGPA 2.0 - 2.6

## Examination Rules
- 40% Continuous Assessment (assignments, tests, quizzes)
- 60% Final Examination
- Minimum 80% lecture attendance required to sit for exams
- Students arriving more than 30 minutes late will not be admitted
- No electronic devices permitted in exam halls
- Cheating results in automatic grade of F and disciplinary action
