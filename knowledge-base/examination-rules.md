# Examination Rules and Procedures 2025/2026

## Examination Eligibility
- Minimum 80% lecture attendance required
- All course assignments and assessments completed
- No outstanding fees
- Valid student ID card required

## Examination Schedule
- Main exams: End of Semester (2 weeks)
- Supplementary exams: 2 weeks after main exam results
- Special exams: Arranged for students with medical reasons or emergencies
- Retake exams: Next semester offering of the course

## Examination Conduct
1. Arrive at least 15 minutes before exam start time
2. Bring valid student ID card - place on desk for verification
3. No phones, smartwatches, or any electronic devices
4. Only permitted materials (calculator, writing tools) on desk
5. No talking or communication with other students
6. No sharing of materials
7. Follow invigilator instructions at all times

## Cheating and Academic Dishonesty
- **First offense**: Grade F for the course + written warning
- **Second offense**: Grade F for all semester courses + suspension for 1 semester
- **Third offense**: Expulsion from the university
- Examples: Plagiarism, copying, unauthorized materials, impersonation, purchasing essays

## Missed Examinations
- Illness: Submit medical certificate within 7 days
- Bereavement: Submit death certificate and letter from parents
- Special exam will be arranged for approved cases
- Unexcused absence: Grade F automatically

## Results
- Released within 4 weeks after the last exam
- Published on student portal
- Grade review requests: Within 14 days of result publication
- Grade review fee: Tsh 30,000 per course
