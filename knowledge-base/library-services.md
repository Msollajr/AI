# Library Services Guide 2025/2026

## Main Library
- Location: Central Campus
- Operating Hours: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM (Mon-Fri), 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Sat-Sun)
- Closed on public holidays

## Borrowing Rules
- Undergraduate students: Up to 5 books for 14 days
- Postgraduate students: Up to 10 books for 28 days
- Staff: Up to 15 books for 30 days
- Renewals: Maximum 2 renewals (online via Library OPAC)
- Reserve materials: 2-hour loan (library use only)
- Reference materials: Not for loan

## Fines
- Overdue books: Tsh 500 per book per day
- Lost books: Replacement cost + Tsh 20,000 processing fee
- Damaged books: 50% of replacement cost
- Unpaid fines: Blocked from borrowing, exam results withheld

## Online Resources
- **OPAC**: library.udsm.ac.tz - Search catalog, renew books
- **E-Journals**: Access via https://library.udsm.ac.tz/ejournals
- **Databases**: JSTOR, ProQuest, ScienceDirect, IEEE, ACM Digital Library
- **Institutional Repository**: https://repository.udsm.ac.tz (theses, dissertations, research papers)
- **Off-campus access**: Requires VPN (instructions on library website)

## Services
- Reference and research assistance: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Inter-library loans: Available for materials not in our collection
- Printing and photocopying: Tsh 100 per page (B&W), Tsh 500 per page (colour)
- Scanning: Free
- Binding services: Tsh 5,000 - 15,000 depending on size
- Study rooms: Available for group study (book in advance)

## Sections
- Ground Floor: Circulation desk, new arrivals, newspapers
- First Floor: Social Sciences and Humanities collection
- Second Floor: Science and Technology collection  
- Third Floor: Postgraduate reading area, e-resources section
- Fourth Floor: Special collections, archives, rare books
