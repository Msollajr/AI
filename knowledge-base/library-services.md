# University of Dar es Salaam Library Services 2025/2026

## Overview
The University of Dar es Salaam Main Library is one of the largest academic libraries in East Africa. It provides access to a wide range of print and digital resources to support teaching, learning, and research.

## Borrowing Privileges
| Category | Books Allowed | Loan Period | Renewals |
|---|---|---|---|
| Undergraduate students | 5 books | 14 days | 2 times |
| Postgraduate students | 10 books | 28 days | 3 times |
| Academic staff | 20 books | 60 days | 3 times |

**How to borrow a book:**
1. Search for the book on the Library OPAC at https://library.udsm.ac.tz
2. Note the call number and shelf location.
3. Take the book to the circulation desk.
4. Present your valid student ID card.

**Online renewals:** Log in to library.udsm.ac.tz → My Account → Renew (up to 2 times per book).

## Fines for Overdue Books
- **Tsh 500 per book per day** overdue (including weekends).
- Students with fines exceeding **Tsh 10,000** are blocked from borrowing.
- Outstanding fines **withhold examination results** if unpaid at semester end.
- Pay fines at the Library Circulation Desk or via the Student Portal.

## Common Questions About Library Services

**Q: How many books can I borrow and for how long?**
A: Undergraduate students can borrow up to 5 books for 14 days (renewable twice online). Postgraduate students can borrow up to 10 books for 28 days.

**Q: How do I access online databases from home?**
A: Install the UDSM VPN from ict.udsm.ac.tz/vpn and log in with your portal credentials. Once connected, all databases (JSTOR, ScienceDirect, IEEE, etc.) are accessible from anywhere.

**Q: I have an overdue fine. How do I pay?**
A: Pay at the Library Circulation Desk or via the Student Portal under Fee Statement. Fines must be cleared before borrowing and before semester results are released.

**Q: How do I book a group study room?**
A: Book online at library.udsm.ac.tz → Room Booking. Rooms hold up to 6 students and are available in 2-hour slots.

**Q: Can I access the library's institutional repository?**
A: Yes. The UDSM Institutional Repository at https://repository.udsm.ac.tz contains theses, dissertations, conference papers, and research reports — all freely accessible without a VPN.
