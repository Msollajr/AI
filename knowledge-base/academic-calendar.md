# Academic Calendar 2025/2026

## Semester 1 (September - January)
- Application for admission: June 1 - August 15
- Orientation week: September 1 - September 5
- Course registration: September 1 - September 19
- Lectures begin: September 8
- Add/Drop deadline: September 19
- Mid-semester break: October 20 - October 26
- Continuous assessment tests: October 27 - November 7
- Late registration closes: November 14 (with penalty)
- Study leave: December 8 - December 14
- Final examinations: December 15 - December 24
- Semester break: December 25 - January 12

## Semester 2 (February - June)
- Course registration: February 2 - February 20
- Lectures begin: February 9
- Add/Drop deadline: February 20
- Mid-semester break: March 30 - April 5
- Continuous assessment tests: April 6 - April 17
- Study leave: May 25 - May 31
- Final examinations: June 1 - June 14
- Semester break: June 15 - August 31

## Supplementary Examinations
- Application: Within 7 days of result publication
- Exams held: July 7 - July 18
- Results released: August 1

## Public Holidays
- Independence Day: December 9
- Christmas: December 25
- Boxing Day: December 26
- New Year: January 1
- Zanzibar Revolution: January 12
- Union Day: April 26
- Labour Day: May 1
- Eid celebrations: As declared by the government
