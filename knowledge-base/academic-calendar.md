# Academic Calendar 2025/2026

## Overview
The University of Dar es Salaam (UDSM) academic year 2025/2026 is divided into two semesters. Each semester consists of approximately 15 teaching weeks, one mid-semester break, one study leave week, and two examination weeks. The full official calendar is published on the UDSM website (www.udsm.ac.tz) under Resources → Academic Calendar.

## Supplementary Examinations
- Students who fail a course with a grade of E or F may apply for supplementary examinations.
- Application deadline: Within 7 days of official result publication.
- Supplementary exams held: July 7 – July 18, 2026.
- Supplementary results released: August 1, 2026.
- Application and fee payment done through the Student Portal (portal.udsm.ac.tz).

## Graduation Ceremonies
- First graduation ceremony: January 2026 (for students who completed in Semester 1).
- Second graduation ceremony: July 2026 (for students who completed in Semester 2).
- Graduation application deadline: 2 months before the ceremony date.

## Public Holidays (No Classes)
- Independence Day: December 9
- Christmas Day: December 25
- Boxing Day: December 26
- New Year's Day: January 1
- Zanzibar Revolution Day: January 12
- Union Day: April 26
- Workers' Day (Labour Day): May 1
- Eid Al-Fitr and Eid Al-Adha: As declared by the government

## Important Registration Deadlines
- Students must complete course registration within the first two weeks of each semester.
- Late registration (Week 3 to Week 6) incurs a penalty fee of Tsh 50,000.
- No registration is accepted after Week 6 under any circumstances.
- Fee payment deadlines: 60% before registration, remaining 40% by Week 8.

## Common Questions About the Academic Calendar

**Q: When does Semester 1 start?**
A: Semester 1 lectures begin on September 8, 2025. Orientation week for new students runs from September 1 to September 5.

**Q: When are the final exams for Semester 2?**
A: Semester 2 final examinations run from June 1 to June 14, 2026.

**Q: When can I register for courses?**
A: Semester 1 registration opens September 1 and the deadline without penalty is September 19. Semester 2 registration opens February 2 and the deadline without penalty is February 20.

**Q: When is the mid-semester break?**
A: Semester 1 mid-semester break is October 20–26. Semester 2 mid-semester break is March 30 – April 5.

**Q: Where can I find the full academic calendar?**
A: The full calendar is available on the official UDSM website at www.udsm.ac.tz under Resources → Academic Calendar, or on the Student Portal at portal.udsm.ac.tz.
