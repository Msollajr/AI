# University of Dar es Salaam Fee Structure 2025/2026

## Tuition Fees per Semester
- Bachelor of Arts: Tsh 1,200,000
- Bachelor of Science: Tsh 1,500,000
- Bachelor of Engineering: Tsh 2,000,000
- Bachelor of Law: Tsh 1,800,000
- Bachelor of Commerce: Tsh 1,400,000
- Bachelor of ICT: Tsh 1,600,000
- Master's Programmes: Tsh 2,500,000 - 4,000,000
- PhD Programmes: Tsh 3,000,000 - 5,000,000

## Other Fees
- Registration Fee: Tsh 50,000 (one-time per academic year)
- Library Fee: Tsh 30,000 per semester
- ICT Fee: Tsh 25,000 per semester
- Sports Fee: Tsh 15,000 per semester
- Medical Fee: Tsh 20,000 per semester
- Examination Fee: Tsh 40,000 per semester
- Student Organization Fee: Tsh 10,000 per semester

## Hostel Fees per Semester
- Single Room: Tsh 600,000
- Shared Room (2 persons): Tsh 400,000 per person
- Shared Room (4 persons): Tsh 300,000 per person

## Payment Options
- Full payment at the beginning of semester
- Two installments: 60% before registration, 40% by Week 8
- Payment methods: Bank deposit, online transfer, mobile money (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa), portal card payment

## Bank Accounts
- NMB Bank: 12310045678 (Tuition Fees)
- CRDB Bank: 02100234567 (Hostel Fees)
- NBC Bank: 01102345678 (Other Fees)

## Penalties
- Late payment penalty: 5% of outstanding amount per month
- Students with outstanding balances will not be allowed to sit for examinations
- Graduating students must clear all fees before graduation
