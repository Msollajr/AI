# Hostel Accommodation Guidelines 2025/2026

## Application Process
1. Applications open online on the Student Portal one month before semester start
2. Pay non-refundable application fee of Tsh 20,000
3. Select preferred hostel block and room type
4. Upload proof of payment
5. Results published 2 weeks before classes commence
6. Accepted students must pay hostel fees within 7 days of acceptance

## Hostel Blocks
- **Hall 1** (Mlimani): First-year students, 4-person rooms
- **Hall 2** (Nkrumah): Second-year students, 2-person rooms  
- **Hall 3** (Nyerere): Senior students, single and 2-person rooms
- **Hall 4** (Kambarage): Postgraduate students, single rooms
- **Hall 5** (UDSM Tower): International students, single rooms with en-suite

## Rules and Regulations
- Quiet hours: 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM (weekdays), 12:00 AM to 8:00 AM (weekends)
- No visitors of the opposite gender after 8:00 PM
- No cooking in rooms (common kitchens available in each hall)
- No alcohol or illegal substances
- No pets allowed
- Guests must register at the reception and leave by 8:00 PM
- Damage to property will be charged to the responsible student

## Check-in / Check-out
- Check-in: 3 days before semester starts
- Check-out: Within 48 hours of last exam
- Late check-out penalty: Tsh 10,000 per day
- Lost key replacement: Tsh 15,000

## Maintenance
- Report maintenance issues to the Hall Supervisor
- Emergency maintenance: 24-hour service
- Room inspection: Once per month by the Hall Supervisor
